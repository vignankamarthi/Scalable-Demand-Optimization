# Data Sources

The primary data source for this project is the ZTBus dataset, a publicly available collection of time-resolved bus driving missions published by ETH Zurich.

**Citation**:
Widmer, E. D., Ritter, L., & Onder, C. H. (2023). ZTBus: A large dataset of time-resolved city bus driving missions. *Scientific Data*, 10, 687. https://doi.org/10.1038/s41597-023-02600-6

**Repository**:
Ammann, M., Kobel, J., Pauli, L., & Gawlikowski, J. (2023). ZTBus: A dataset of second-resolved driving missions of inner-city transit buses. *ETH Zurich Research Collection*.
https://www.research-collection.ethz.ch/entities/researchdata/61ac2f6e-2ca9-4229-8242-aed3b0c0d47c

The dataset is distributed under a Creative Commons license and is available for download in CSV format. The full dataset is approximately 8.5 GB (compressed: 1.9 GB), with an 84 MB sample subset available for initial exploration.
